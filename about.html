T-Level Studies

What a T-Level Actually Means

T-Levels are a rigorous technical qualification designed in collaboration with industry. Unlike traditional A-Levels, they combine classroom learning with real industry placement, making them uniquely suited to someone who wants to enter the tech industry directly.

My course has given me a broad foundation — from understanding object-oriented programming in Python and JavaScript, to designing and normalising relational databases, to applying agile methodologies in team projects. The industry placement requirement means I've had to perform professionally, not just academically.
